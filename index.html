About Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa and is located on the western coast of the continent. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, with major groups including Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. Nigeria is also known for its diverse wildlife and vibrant music and film industries, particularly Nollywood. The country is a leading producer of oil and is home to natural landmarks like Zuma Rock and the Niger River.
